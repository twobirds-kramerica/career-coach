✨What you can do in this beta

  • ✓ CV analysis against real job postings.Paste a job description and your CV — Career Coach scores your fit and tells you exactly where you stand.
  • ✓ Gap identification.See which skills or experiences the employer is looking for that aren't clearly visible in your CV yet.
  • ✓ Tailored cover letter drafts.Generate a starting-point cover letter matched to the role — yours to edit and personalise.
  • ✓ Interview question prep.Get a list of likely questions based on the job description, with coaching on how to approach each one.
  • ✓ Multi-job tracking.Save and compare multiple applications in one place — no spreadsheet required.
