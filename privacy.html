Privacy Policy

Career Coach by Two Birds Innovation  |  Last updated: June 2026

Plain language summary: Career Coach runs entirely in your browser. Your job data, your API key, and your cover letters are stored only on your device. They are never sent to Two Birds Innovation. The only data that leaves your browser is what you explicitly send to your AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, or another). We do not have accounts, cookies, or analytics.

What We Do Not Collect

Two Birds Innovation does not collect, receive, or store any of the following:

  • Your name or contact details
  • Your job postings or application materials
  • Your API key
  • Your cover letters or resume content
  • Any usage data or analytics

Career Coach has no backend server, no database, and no user accounts.

What Is Stored on Your Device

Your browser's localStorage (storage space on your own device) saves:

  • Your AI provider selection and API key
  • Job postings you have added
  • Application scores and coaching notes from AI analysis
  • Cover letters generated during your session
  • Your settings and preferences (language, etc.)

This data is stored entirely on your device and never sent to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using your browser's history settings.

API Calls to Your AI Provider

When you ask Career Coach to analyse a job posting or generate a cover letter, your browser sends a request directly to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, or another). This request includes the job description text and prompts we have written. It does not pass through our servers.

Your API key is sent directly from your browser to the provider. Two Birds Innovation never sees or stores your key. Review your provider's privacy policy for details on how they handle your requests:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Cookies

Career Coach does not use cookies.

Analytics

Career Coach does not use Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, or any other analytics service. We have no visibility into how you use the tool.

Children

Career Coach is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from people under 18.

Your Rights Under PIPEDA

As a Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) matter: because we collect no personal information about you, there is no data for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. Your data exists only on your own device, where you have full control.
